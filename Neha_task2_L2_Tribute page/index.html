Ratan Tata

1937 - 2024

"Take the stones people throw at you and use them to build a monument."

Biography

Ratan Tata was one of India's most respected industrialists and philanthropists. He served as the chairman of Tata Group and transformed it into a global business organization. Under his leadership, Tata Group expanded internationally and introduced innovative ideas that contributed to India's growth. Ratan Tata was admired for his simplicity, leadership, kindness, and dedication toward social welfare. His journey inspires millions of people to work hard and dream big.
